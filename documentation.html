Frequently Asked Questions

How do I connect with a mentor?

Use the Community Matchmaking feature to search for advisors based on expertise and focus area. You can filter by specific skills and connect directly with community members who can help you.

Can I suggest new features?

Yes! Visit the Community Hub and navigate to the Roadmap section to submit new ideas and vote on existing ones. Your input helps shape the future of WorldPulse.

How do I request training?

Go to the Training section in the Community Hub and submit a request for the specific training you need. Other community members can then offer to provide that training.

How are impact stories recognized?

Impact stories are shared in the Community Hub where other members can read, vote, and celebrate achievements. Outstanding stories may be featured in our newsletter and social media.
